Desalination plant

500-2-db8sw250-500m³/day.

High Pressure pumps with energy recuperator for desalination

overview product range data sheets related product

The axial-plunger HP pump with integrated energy recovery was created without tubes and pipelines. All four channels are placed on the pump back panel. APPR series axial-plunger pump is made of Duplex and Super Duplex steel. The pump has no rolling bearings. All the friction pairs are made of Super Duplex steel and PEEK polymer. Seawater feeding is performed 50% by plungers moving with the rotor from electric drive along the inclined disc, and 50% by mobile hubs (made of PEEK) installed on the plungers. The hubs are driven by the pressure of high-pressure brine bled from the membrane elements.

The PEEK covered distribution disc with four channels distributes the fluid flows. During module development, the necessity arose to make the membrane body not so conventional. To achieve the normal operation in absence of tubes all three channels (high-pressure water, brine, permeate) were placed on the front panel of the body.
